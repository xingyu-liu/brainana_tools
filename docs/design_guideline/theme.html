Refined Instrument · Variant B

Graphite + Amber — a warm lab instrument

Warm graphite neutrals — grey with a deliberate warm bias — under a single amber accent that carries forward today's signature gold. Softer contrast, gentle panel elevation, and a humanist UI face make it the most comfortable of the three for long imaging sessions in a dim room. Data still sits in monospace with tabular figures; the warmth never costs precision.

The amber accent and the gold region color are deliberately close but distinct — accent leans orange (interaction), region gold stays yellow (meaning). Everything warm is anchored by the same graphite neutral so the palette reads as one family.
